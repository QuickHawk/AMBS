Can you tell me what service is offered by each type of ambulance?
An Emergency Ambulance is equipped to provide advance life support to the patient who is in critical situation and demanding medical assistance at the earliest. On the other hand, the Non-Emergency Ambulance will be equipped to serve the people who are in stable health condition but unable to travel in regular vehicles for various reasons.
